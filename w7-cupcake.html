Cupcake

A cupcake is a small cake that serves one person and is baked in a small thin paper or aluminum cup.

Ingedients

  • 1 1/2 cup Flour
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking powder

Procedures

  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. Combine Butter and Sugar
  3. Add Eggs and Vanilla
  4. Alternate Adding Dry and Wet Ingredients
  5. Fill the Cupcake Liners
  6. Bake and Cool Cupcakes
